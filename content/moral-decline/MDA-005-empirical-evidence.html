What caused P to cross below Pc in 1968–1973? No single event caused the collapse. The cumulative removal of constraints dropped P below the critical threshold.

The collapse of America's moral architecture between 1968 and 1973 was a phase transition — not a cultural drift, not a generational preference shift, not an inevitable evolution.[51?]
